ABOUT US

Wilfully was born out of the experience of Lisa Uible, one of Wilfully’s cofounders. Following the unexpected death of a close relative, Lisa saw first-hand the trauma that already-distraught relatives suffered because of a lack of estate planning. When she later tried to create her own will, Lisa realized there was a lack of simple, easy-to-use, free online one-stop-shops for estate planning. Determined to come up with something simple that covered the most important questions without incurring legal fees, Lisa put her experience to use and started Wilfully.
